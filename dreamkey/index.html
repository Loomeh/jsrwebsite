What is Dream Key?

Dream Key is the name of the internet browser disc which allows you and your Sega Dreamcast access to the Jet Grind Radio Homepage. If you're ready to email, check out hints and tips for your Jet Grind Radio, and keep up to date with daily Jet Grind Radio news then this is the place for you!
