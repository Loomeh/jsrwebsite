3. Connecting the Dreamcast to a telephone line

Plug one end of the supplied white modem cable into the Sega Dreamcast modem and connect the other end to the supplied telephone adapter. This adapter plugs into your telephone line socket. Even if you don't connect a Sega Dreamcast keyboard or memory card (Visual Memory Unit, sold separately), you can still use Dream Key.
